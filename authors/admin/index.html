Oleg Zendel

PhD candidate in Computer Science

RMIT University

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate under the guidance of Prof. J. Shane Culpepper and Prof. Falk Scholer at the RMIT University in Melbourne, Australia. My current research focuses on different aspects of query performance prediction (QPP).

Previously, I’ve completed an M.Sc. at the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Oren Kurland where I examined how to improve QPP by using reference queries.

Along with my research, I enjoy the company of Quan – a Guide Dog in training, whom we’re raising as part of a volunteering program.
