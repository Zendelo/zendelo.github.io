About

I am currently a PhD candidate at the RMIT University, Melbourne, Australia. Under the guidance of Prof. J. Shane Culpepper and Prof. Falk Scholer.

I completed my M.Sc. at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Oren Kurland where I examined how to improve query performance prediction using reference queries.

My current research focuses on the evaluation of query performance prediction.
